The Basic Tic Tac Toe Rules

Players: Two players, one uses "X" and the other "O".

Objective: Be the first to get three of your marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

Gameplay:
  • Players take turns placing their mark (X or O) in an empty cell.
  • The game starts with an empty board.

    • Winning: The first player to align three of their marks in a row wins.

    Draw: If all nine cells are filled and neither player has three in a row, the game ends in a draw.
